FAQ

Transparent answers for you and your family.

  • What are dues?

    Semester dues cover chapter operation, archon events, and materials. We'll share the exact amount at info night. Payment plans are available—we don't want finances to be a barrier to the right fit.

  • What's the time commitment?

    We expect brothers to prioritize academics, then Archon. Typical commitments: weekly chapter meeting, one or two events per month, and participation in at least one community project per semester. We work around class and work schedules.

  • What's the new member process like?

    New members go through an education period where you learn our history, values, and expectations. It includes brotherhood events that will turn you into an official Archon brother. It's designed to help you grow and connect—not to embarrass or harm anyone. Length and requirements follow Rio Grande university guidelines.

  • What's your stance on hazing?

    Archon has a zero-tolerance anti-hazing policy. We do not haze. Period. Our member process is built on respect, mentorship, and brotherhood—not humiliation or danger. We're proud to work with our university to keep our fraternity safe and welcoming. If you ever have concerns, we encourage you to reach out to our leadership or the Rio Grande university.
